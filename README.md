# 📊 AtliQ Hardware Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AtliQ Hardware Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance across multiple markets, customers, products, and sales channels.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI monitoring, and dynamic filtering, enabling stakeholders to make informed business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

AtliQ Hardware operates across multiple countries with hundreds of products and customers. Monitoring business performance using raw transactional data is time-consuming and inefficient.

This dashboard was developed to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Analyze market and customer performance
- Identify top-performing products
- Support strategic business decisions

## 📈 Key Insights

- India emerged as the highest-performing market.
- APAC contributed the largest regional sales.
- Accessories generated the highest sales among product segments.
- Mouse was the leading product category.
- Direct and Distributor channels contributed significantly to total sales.
- Sales remained stable across fiscal years.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing markets.
- Optimize underperforming product categories.
- Strengthen relationships with high-value customers.
- Continue KPI monitoring through interactive dashboards.
- Leverage data-driven insights for business planning.
